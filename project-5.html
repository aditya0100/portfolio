Yaad Plug

Yaad Plug is a cross-platform mobile application developed using Flutter, catering to both Android and iOS users. It offers convenient services including meals delivery, courier services, and freight transportation. The app features separate logins for users and riders, ensuring a seamless experience tailored to each user's needs.

Project Overview

Users can browse and select meals from a list of available options, add items to their cart, and place orders. They have the flexibility to specify their delivery location on a map and track their order's status in real-time using the order history feature, which displays the route between the rider and the user. Users can also request courier services through the app, facilitating quick and reliable delivery of packages. For larger shipments, users can request freight services to transport goods efficiently. The app supports authentication via Google Sign-In and Apple Sign-In, providing users with a hassle-free login experience. On the other hand, Riders have access to a list of active orders, allowing them to efficiently manage their deliveries. They can update the status of orders using a slider button, providing real-time updates to users. Riders can navigate to the delivery location using a map view, ensuring they reach their destination without any hassle.

Yaad Plug represents a comprehensive solution for individuals seeking convenient and reliable services, including meals delivery, courier services, and freight transportation. With its user-friendly interface, real-time tracking capabilities, and efficient order management system, the app aims to streamline the process of availing various services while ensuring a seamless experience for both users and service providers.
