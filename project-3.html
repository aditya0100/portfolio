Project Overview

Imagine an app that's super easy to use and helps you with everyday issues in your community. Well, this app, created with Flutter and powered by Firebase, is just that. First off, it's safe you log in securely whenever you need to. Then, the app lets you check out what's going on around you in two cool ways either through lists or on a map right inside the app. But here's the really neat part: if you come across a new issue, like a pothole or a broken streetlight, you can report it right from the app. You describe the problem in a few words, add some pictures if you want, and even tag the exact location. It's like giving your community a heads-up about things that need fixing.

The app looks and feels smooth because of Flutter, and Firebase makes sure everything you do is safe and happens in real-time. So, it's not just an app; it's your helpful sidekick for making your neighborhood a better place.
